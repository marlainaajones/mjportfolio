Strategy: Led the development of the comprehensive messaging framework including branding, messaging strategy, subsite development, and employee story videos. Ensured foundational messaging was adapted across multiple internal channels.

Innovation: Transformed isolated campus events into system-wide engagement opportunities, creating sustainable framework for ongoing DEI communications.

I-9 Compliance Crisis Communication

Challenge: Reverify I-9 forms for 14,500 employees in just 2.5 months to meet COVID compliance requirements by August 30 deadline.

Strategy: Developed multi-channel communication campaign with constant touchpoints including email communications, training, text alerts, portal communications, and manager resources.

Execution: Created comprehensive communication timeline ensuring consistent messaging across all channels while managing time-sensitive compliance requirements.

Crisis Management Success:

  • Achieved 100% compliance by August 30 deadline
  • Reached 14,500 employees with clear instructions
  • Implemented multi-channel crisis communication strategy
